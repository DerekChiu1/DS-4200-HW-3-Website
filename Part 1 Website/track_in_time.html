Track in Time

This page contains detailed information about the song "Track in Time."

About the author:

Dennis Kuo is an American doctor and musician who makes beautiful, soothing piano music that are relaxing and calm. He claims that through music and medicine, he hopes to be the person that contributes positivity in the world. Asides from track in time, Dennis also produces many other piano song, which are all very good as well, such as "Last walk in the rain" and "World before time". About the piano piece:

Track in time is a song made by Dennis Kuo in 2018, which was uploaded on his Youtube channel and can be found on spotify as well. Track in time's melody is very gentle, soft, and soothing, while carrying a sense of nostalgia and tranquility. Many listeners state that the song feels like a musician reflecting by himself on the time and good memories passed, under a chilling atmosphere. A lot of people have made piano and guitar cover of this song online, which showcases people's love for this song over the internet.
