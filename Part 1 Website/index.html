Welcome to My Personal Website!

In this website, you'll learn about my favorite hobby - playing piano

I've learned piano for 9 years from 9 to 17 years old, in which I stopped going to the piano class in my last year in high school. It's not because I don't like it anymore, it's just because the workload became more heavy and I was busy with university applications. I certainly still love piano a lot and I continued learning it by myself by doing self practice in my free time, where I picked the songs I like and played them using online piano sheets. As I was one of a few people in my class who knows how to play piano, I often got asked or invited to play the keyboard for school events, including the foreign language singing contest and my middle school and high school graduation ceremonies. I enjoy playing in a band or friend group for school events, as I can showcase my skill to our school's crowd, although I always got really nervous before the show. In the following website, you can look at some piano songs I've played in the past with screenshots of my recordings and link to the ful video, which are mainly J-pop or Chinese songs. My next goal is to play a piece called "Track in time" by Dennis Kuo, and the detail info about that song can be accessed by clicking the button at the end of its description, which should take you to another page.

Cover 1: Piano duel from movie Secret

This cover is about a piano duel clip from an old movie called Secret, which is an alternation piece from Chopin's Waltz.
